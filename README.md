### About Me

Senior Data Scientist & ML Engineer at **niologic GmbH** (Germany). Previously ML Researcher at **Jaeger Gruppe** (3.5 years), building production NLP and computer vision systems. Published researcher in multimodal NLP for the construction industry ([CIB W78 2024](https://itc.scix.net/pdfs/w78-2024-paper_112.pdf)).

- M.Sc. Applied Computer Science, **Ruhr University Bochum** (Grade: 1.9)
- B.Sc. Internet of Things Engineering, **Beijing University of Technology**
- Languages: Chinese (native), German (fluent), English (fluent)

### Experience

**Senior Data Scientist & ML Engineer** — niologic GmbH *(2025 – present)*
> Production data solutions: ARR modeling in Azure SQL & dbt with anomaly detection; Airflow-based reconciliation systems for Stripe/PayPal/Skrill.

**ML Researcher | CV & NLP Engineer** — Jaeger Gruppe *(2021 – 2025)*
